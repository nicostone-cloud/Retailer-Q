Step 2 of 3
Review & edit
Each question shows why it was included. Edit inline, mark what you know, remove anything irrelevant.
What to do with responses when they come back
Gate 1 responses
Confirm sponsor and programme owner. Add to Programme Directory. Steerco cannot proceed without named contacts on both sides.
Gate 2 responses
DPIA, CAPEX, hardware approval, IT security. Any unanswered Gate 2 critical is a blocker. Start DPIA and hardware approval before contracts are signed.
Gate 3 responses
SLAs, installation access, contractor approval. All must be resolved before pilot launches. Do not go live without signed SLAs.
Gate 4 responses
Media sales capability and brand commitments. Secure at least one confirmed Day-1 brand before go-live.
Sensor responses
Camera acceptance unlocks age/gender and zone data. BLE acceptance unlocks verified impressions. Each sensor needs separate privacy and hardware sign-off.
Data responses
EPOS at SKU level unlocks sales uplift (Level 4–5). Share data granularity answer with Jonathan Stafford immediately to set measurement expectations.
